About Blueprint Smiles

Built on Gratitude, Growth, and Accountability

Blueprint Smiles is a doctor-led dental support organization proudly serving the greater Atlanta area. Built on a foundation of gratitude, growth, and accountability, the company helps dental practices run smoothly so clinicians can focus on what matters most, delivering exceptional patient care.

Through high-quality, affordable dental services and innovative practice management, Blueprint Smiles makes dentistry feel comfortable and accessible for every patient. The organization believes continuous improvement is key, always looking for better ways to support practices and enhance the patient experience.

Blueprint Smiles also invests deeply in its team members, creating a culture where work and life can thrive together. From supporting local communities to empowering dental professionals, the company is committed to making a real difference beyond the dental chair.

Services

Care for Every Stage of Your Smile

Practices in the Blueprint Smiles network offer a full range of dependable dental care, built around comfort and affordability.

General Dentistry

Routine checkups, cleanings, fillings, and preventive care that keeps the whole family's smile healthy year-round.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers designed to help patients feel confident every time they smile.

Advanced & Restorative Care

Crowns, bridges, root canals, and dental implants, backed by experienced specialists for more complex needs.

Our Mission

Our mission at Blueprint Smiles is to support dental practices across the greater Atlanta area so they can deliver exceptional, patient-centered care every day. We do this by handling the operational side of dentistry, including staffing, scheduling, and practice management, so clinicians can stay focused on their patients. We are committed to making dental care accessible through high-quality, affordable services guided by our core values of gratitude, growth, and accountability. We also invest in the people who make great care possible, supporting a healthy work-life balance for our teams and giving back to the local Atlanta community. One patient, one practice, and one smile at a time, our mission is to make dentistry feel simple, comfortable, and dependable for everyone we serve.
